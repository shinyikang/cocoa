In “Cocoa”, you get to experience first-hand the different hardships cocoa farmers face. As a cocoa farmer, you start with 9 plots of land, 3 wellness hearts, and 10 dollars. Your duty is to sustainably cultivate your plantation, sell cocoa beans, and survive.

Along the way, you’ll also get to “unwrap” interesting facts about chocolate.

Ready to play?

The chocolate production system has a long-standing history of exploiting cocoa farmers and harming the environment.

Having to live off of less than $1 a day, cocoa farmers in West Africa burn forests, enslave their own children, and engage in hazardous work to sustain their livelihoods and their families.

Sweet, bitter, sometimes spicy, sometimes fruity… chocolate is a sweet delight that brings us joy.

However, did you know that there is a dark, broken side to chocolate?
